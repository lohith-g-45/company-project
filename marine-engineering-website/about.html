Company History

From Vision to Reality

Founded in 2003, Marine Engineering & Shipbuilding was established with a singular vision: to become a world-class provider of innovative marine engineering and shipbuilding solutions. What started as a small design firm has grown into a comprehensive marine services company serving clients across the globe.

Our Journey

  • 2003: Company founded with 25 employees
  • 2008: Opened first shipyard facility with 200+ workforce
  • 2012: Achieved ISO 9001 certification
  • 2015: Expanded to three locations across India
  • 2018: Completed 100th ship project milestone
  • 2020: Established International division
  • 2023: Launched green technology initiative
  • 2024: New state-of-the-art facilities opened

Built on Reliability

Every phase of our growth has focused on quality, long-term partnerships, and engineering discipline.
